Avocado & Strawberry Smoothie

This smoothie combines creamy avocado with strawberries and bananas for a refreshing and filling beverage.

Smoothies are perfect for a quick snack or breakfast. This one is quick and easy to make.

Frozen strawberries and bananas work as fell as fresh.

Ingredients:

  • 1/3 avocado
  • 12 strawberries
  • 1/2 banana
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1/2 cup ice

Steps:

  1. Put all the ingredients in a blender and mix until creamy.
  2. Adjust the ratio of ice to water to get the thickness of smoothie you prefer.
