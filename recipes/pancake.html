American Pancakes

Easy, American-style, fluffy pancakes. Top with something sweet like fruit or syrup.

Ingredients:

  • 200g plain flour
  • 10g baking powder
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tbsp sea salt
  • 300 ml milk
  • 40 g butter (melted)
  • 1 egg

Steps:

  1. Sift flour into a large mixing bowl, add baking powder, sugar & salt. Mix and make a well, then add milk, butter and 1 egg. Gently whisk all the ingredients together until you have a slightly thick consistency.
  2. Preheat a frying pan or griddle over medium heat. Pour a small ladle of batter onto the pan
  3. Cook until the batter has thickened and small bubbles begin to show, this will take about one minute. Flip and continue to cook for 30 seconds, until the underside has browned.
